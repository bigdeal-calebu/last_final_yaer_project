Camera Resolution

Capture at high resolution (720p, 1080p, or higher)

cap.set(cv2.CAP_PROP_FRAME_WIDTH, 1280)

cap.set(cv2.CAP_PROP_FRAME_HEIGHT, 720)

2. Frame Rate (FPS)

Set FPS for smoother motion (cap.set(cv2.CAP_PROP_FPS, 30))

High FPS reduces motion blur

3. Camera Focus and Exposure

Control focus if supported

Control exposure: cap.set(cv2.CAP_PROP_EXPOSURE, value)

4. Camera Encoding (FOURCC)

Use MJPG or YUYV for higher-quality video stream

cap.set(cv2.CAP_PROP_FOURCC, cv2.VideoWriter_fourcc(*'MJPG'))

5. Brightness and Contrast Adjustment

Adjust using cv2.convertScaleAbs(frame, alpha, beta)

Enhances visibility in dark or overly bright scenes

6. Gamma Correction

Corrects under/overexposed frames

gamma = 1.2
lookUpTable = np.array([((i/255.0)**(1/gamma))*255 for i in np.arange(0,256)]).astype("uint8")
frame = cv2.LUT(frame, lookUpTable)
7. Noise Reduction

cv2.fastNlMeansDenoisingColored() for color frames

cv2.GaussianBlur() or cv2.bilateralFilter() for edge-preserving smoothing

8. Sharpening

Using kernel convolution (cv2.filter2D)

Makes edges crisper and details clearer

9. Histogram Equalization

Improves contrast in grayscale frames (cv2.equalizeHist())

Use CLAHE for color images (cv2.createCLAHE())

10. White Balance Correction

Ensures natural color tones

Adjust channels based on lighting

11. Color Space Conversion

Convert to appropriate color space (BGR→RGB, BGR→LAB) for better processing

Helps with contrast and color correction

12. CLAHE (Contrast Limited Adaptive Histogram Equalization)

Prevents over-enhancement in bright areas

Gives more uniform contrast

13. Resize with Proper Interpolation

Use cv2.INTER_CUBIC for upscaling

Use cv2.INTER_AREA for downscaling

Avoids pixelation

14. Real-time Image Stabilization

Reduce shake using optical flow (cv2.calcOpticalFlowPyrLK)

Stabilized frames appear sharper

15. Dynamic Range/HDR

Merge multiple exposures for high contrast scenes

Reduces blown-out highlights

16. Edge Enhancement

Using Laplacian filter or sharpening kernel

Makes details stand out

17. Dithering & Anti-aliasing

Reduces jagged edges in scaled frames

18. Threaded Video Capture

Use multithreading to separate frame capture and processing

Increases FPS and reduces lag

19. GPU Acceleration

Use OpenCV CUDA modules for filters and processing

Faster processing = smoother, high-quality video

20. Proper Lighting

Not OpenCV-specific, but crucial

Good lighting reduces noise and improves color fidelity
